# Cold Outreach Methodology

Signal-based prospecting framework for outbound that doesn't feel like outbound. Research-before-reach, personalization depth tiers, and timing optimization.

---

## Signal-Based Prospecting

Never reach out cold. Reach out warm - with a signal.

**Tier 1 signals (strongest - act within 24h):**
- Job posting for role your product fills (hiring = budget allocated)
- Funding announcement (new capital = new spending)
- Executive hire in your buyer persona's function
- Competitor churned or had public incident
- Engaged with your content (downloaded, commented, shared)

**Tier 2 signals (strong - act within 7 days):**
- Tech stack change visible on BuiltWith/Wappalyzer
- Company expansion (new office, new market)
- Industry regulation change affecting their business
- Conference speaker/attendee in your space
- Published content about the problem you solve

**Tier 3 signals (ambient - use for personalization, not timing):**
- Company size/growth rate fits ICP
- Industry vertical match
- Geographic fit
- Existing tech stack compatibility

**Rule: never send without at least one Tier 2+ signal.** Tier 3 alone is spray-and-pray.

## Research-Before-Reach

Minimum research before first touch:
1. Company website (what they do, how they position)
2. Target contact's LinkedIn (recent posts, role duration, background)
3. Signal source (the event that triggered outreach)
4. Competitive landscape (who they might be using now)

This takes 5-10 minutes per prospect manually. Scout automates 80% of this.

## Personalization Depth Tiers

**Light personalization (Tier 3 signals only):**
- Company name + industry reference
- Generic pain point for their segment
- Appropriate when volume matters more than precision
- Expected reply rate: 2-5%

**Medium personalization (Tier 2 signals):**
- Specific signal reference ("Saw you're hiring a [role]...")
- Pain point connected to their situation
- One piece of social proof relevant to their industry
- Expected reply rate: 8-15%

**Deep personalization (Tier 1 signals):**
- Signal + business context + specific insight
- Reference to their content, strategy, or public statements
- Tailored value proposition for their exact situation
- Expected reply rate: 15-30%

## Timing Principles

- **Tuesday-Thursday** for B2B email (Monday inbox overwhelm, Friday checkout)
- **Morning send** (7-9am recipient's timezone) for decision-makers
- **Mid-afternoon** (2-4pm) for individual contributors
- **Within 24h of signal** for Tier 1 events - speed is the differentiator
- **Never send on holidays** or the day before a long weekend
