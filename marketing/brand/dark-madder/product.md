# Dark Madder - Product

## Product Description
Dark Madder (dm.kinetiks.ai / darkmadder.com) is an AI content engine
built on a learning architecture. It researches, plans, generates, publishes,
and then learns from the results - creating a compounding content intelligence
that improves with every cycle. Connected to Kinetiks ID for deep brand
context, Dark Madder produces content that sounds like your best writer
and gets sharper every month.

## Key Features

### 1. Voice Engine
Trained on your brand's actual voice profile from Kinetiks ID. Not a tone
slider - a deep voice model that captures vocabulary, sentence structure,
personality, and communication patterns. Produces content that passes the
"did a human write this?" test.

### 2. Research & Planner
Strategic layer before generation. Includes:
- Topic clustering and gap analysis
- Competitive content audit
- Search intent mapping
- Content calendar generation
- Angle and hook development

### 3. Content Generator
Multi-format generation powered by Voice Engine context:
- Long-form blog posts and articles
- Social media posts and threads
- Email sequences and newsletters
- Landing page copy
- Ad copy and variations

### 4. Learning Loop
The core differentiator. After content is published, the Learning Loop:
- Tracks engagement metrics and performance data
- Identifies which voice patterns, angles, and formats perform best
- Feeds insights back into the Voice Engine and Research modules
- Creates a compounding knowledge base that sharpens every output
- Surfaces actionable recommendations for future content strategy

### 5. Splits Engine
Built-in content variant testing:
- Generate 2-5 variations of any content piece
- A/B test headlines, hooks, CTAs, and angles
- Route winners back into the Learning Loop
- Build a data-backed understanding of what resonates

### 6. Framer CMS Integration
Direct publishing pipeline to Framer:
- Content flows from generator to published page
- Formatting, images, and metadata handled automatically
- No manual copy-paste or reformatting required

## Integrations
- **Kinetiks ID**: Voice profile, brand context, audience data (native)
- **Framer**: Direct CMS publishing (native)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, Plausible for Learning Loop data
- **Social**: Buffer, LinkedIn for distribution tracking
- **SEO**: Ahrefs, Clearscope data import for Research module

Dark Madder is the only content engine with a true learning architecture.
Every publish cycle makes the next one smarter. After 90 days of use,
the engine knows your voice, your audience's preferences, and your
highest-performing patterns better than any human content strategist could.
