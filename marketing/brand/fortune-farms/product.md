# Fortune Farms - Product

## Product Description
Fortune Farms is a self-contained B2B cold outreach ecosystem built for
founders and small sales teams. Four specialized tools work together to
cover the complete outbound workflow: Bloomify captures contacts with 1
click, Greenhaus builds buyer personas, Plantr identifies target companies,
and Harvest manages sequences and pipeline. Designed for teams that need
results without enterprise complexity.

## The Four Tools

### 1. Bloomify (Chrome Extension)
One-click contact capture from anywhere on the web:
- Browser extension that overlays on LinkedIn, company websites, directories
- Single click captures name, title, company, email, phone
- Auto-enrichment fills in missing data points
- Captured contacts flow directly into Harvest pipeline
- Works on any page with identifiable contact information
- Zero data entry. Browse the web. Build your list.

### 2. Greenhaus (Persona Builder)
Build detailed buyer personas that drive targeting and messaging:
- Guided persona creation with structured prompts
- Define demographics, motivations, objections, and buying triggers
- Pain point mapping tied to your product's value propositions
- Persona cards that inform Plantr targeting and Harvest messaging
- Multiple personas for different buyer types and segments
- Syncs with Kinetiks ID Audience Layer when connected

### 3. Plantr (Target Company Identification)
Find and prioritize the companies worth pursuing:
- Company identification based on ICP criteria from Greenhaus personas
- Firmographic filtering: size, industry, location, technology, funding
- Company scoring and prioritization
- Target account lists with company intelligence
- Integration with Bloomify for contact discovery within target companies
- Export to Harvest for sequence enrollment

### 4. Harvest CRM (Pipeline & Sequences)
Manage outreach and deals from first email to close:
- Contact management with full enrichment data from Bloomify
- Multi-step email sequences with personalization
- Pipeline stages and deal tracking
- Reply detection and sequence pausing
- Basic reporting on open rates, reply rates, and meetings booked
- Shared pipeline for small team collaboration

## How They Work Together
1. **Greenhaus** defines who you're looking for (persona)
2. **Plantr** finds the companies that match (targeting)
3. **Bloomify** captures the contacts at those companies (prospecting)
4. **Harvest** sends the sequences and manages the pipeline (outreach + CRM)

Each tool works independently but compounds when used together.

## Integrations
- **Kinetiks ID**: Greenhaus syncs personas with Audience Layer (optional)
- **Email**: Gmail, Outlook for sequence sending
- **LinkedIn**: Bloomify overlays on LinkedIn pages
- **Calendar**: Google Calendar for meeting booking links
- **Export**: CSV export from any tool

## Unique Value
Fortune Farms is the only outbound system designed specifically for founders
and small teams. 4 tools, 1 ecosystem, zero enterprise bloat. Install
Bloomify, build a persona in Greenhaus, target companies in Plantr, and
send your first sequence from Harvest - all in the same afternoon.
