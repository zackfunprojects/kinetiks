# Kinetiks ID - Audience

## Primary Persona: "The Overwhelmed Marketing Lead"

### Demographics
- **Title**: VP Marketing, Head of Growth, Director of Marketing Ops
- **Company size**: 50-500 employees (scale-up stage)
- **Industry**: B2B SaaS, professional services, tech-enabled businesses
- **Experience**: 5-12 years in marketing, 2-4 years in current role
- **Age range**: 28-42
- **Reports to**: CMO or CEO directly

### Day-to-Day Reality
- Manages 8-15 marketing tools that don't talk to each other
- Spends 30% of time re-entering the same brand info into different tools
- Has a "brand guidelines" doc that nobody reads and is 6 months outdated
- Onboards a new tool every quarter and dreads the setup process
- Gets inconsistent outputs from AI tools because context is always incomplete

### Pain Points (Ranked)
1. **Tool fragmentation**: Every tool has its own version of "who we are"
2. **Context loss**: AI outputs are generic because they lack business depth
3. **Onboarding drag**: New tools take 2-4 weeks to produce useful output
4. **Consistency breakdown**: Sales says one thing, marketing says another,
   the website says a third
5. **Maintenance burden**: Brand info changes but updating 12 tools is a project

### Sophistication Level
- **Technical**: Comfortable with SaaS, APIs, integrations. Not a developer.
- **Marketing**: Deep understanding of positioning, funnels, content strategy.
- **AI literacy**: Uses ChatGPT and AI writing tools regularly. Understands
  the "garbage in, garbage out" problem. Wants better input, not better prompts.

### What They Value
- Structured thinking and frameworks
- Proof over promises
- Time saved on operational tasks
- Tools that compound in value over time
- Clean interfaces that respect their intelligence

### Where They Hang Out
- **LinkedIn**: Primary professional network, active in marketing groups
- **Slack communities**: Exit Five, Demand Curve, Pavilion
- **Podcasts**: Lenny's Podcast, Marketing Against the Grain, Everyone Hates Marketers
- **Events**: SaaStr, Pavilion events, HubSpot INBOUND
- **Content**: Read Reforge, follow marketing ops thought leaders

## Secondary Persona: "The Founder-Marketer"
- CEO or co-founder at a 10-50 person company doing their own marketing
- Less sophisticated in marketing frameworks but highly strategic
- Needs structure imposed on their scattered brand thinking
- Values the Cartographer operator for guided intake
- Price-sensitive but willing to pay for time savings
