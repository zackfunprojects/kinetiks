# Kinetiks ID - Product

## Product Description
Kinetiks ID (id.kinetiks.ai) is the intelligence hub at the center of
the Kinetiks ecosystem. Users build a structured business identity through
the 8-layer Context Structure, guided by three specialized AI Operators.
This identity then powers every other product in the Kinetiks stack -
content generation, outbound, landing pages, and PR - ensuring consistency
and depth across every marketing touchpoint.

## The 8-Layer Context Structure
1. **Brand Layer**: Mission, values, origin story, brand personality
2. **Audience Layer**: ICPs, personas, segments, buying behavior
3. **Market Layer**: Industry dynamics, trends, market size, timing
4. **Competitor Layer**: Direct/indirect competitors, positioning gaps
5. **Voice Layer**: Tone, vocabulary, communication style, examples
6. **Positioning Layer**: Differentiation, value props, messaging hierarchy
7. **Product Layer**: Features, benefits, use cases, technical details
8. **Goals Layer**: OKRs, campaign objectives, growth targets, timelines

## Key Features

### 1. Three Specialized Operators
- **Cartographer**: Guides initial identity intake. Asks structured questions,
  maps your business landscape, and populates the 8 layers systematically.
- **Archivist**: Monitors data quality, flags outdated information, suggests
  updates, and maintains the integrity of your identity over time.
- **Marcus**: Conversational interface for querying and updating your identity.
  Ask questions, get insights, make changes through natural dialogue.

### 2. Ecosystem Integration
Every Kinetiks product pulls from ID automatically. Update your positioning
in ID and Dark Madder adjusts content tone. Add a competitor in ID and
Litmus monitors their press coverage. One change propagates everywhere.

### 3. Living Identity
The Context Structure is not a static document. It evolves as Operators
detect market shifts, competitor moves, and internal changes. The Archivist
proactively surfaces what needs updating.

### 4. Guided Onboarding
The Cartographer runs a structured intake session that builds your full
identity in one sitting. No blank-page problem. No guessing what to fill in.

### 5. Context API
Other tools (inside or outside Kinetiks) can pull structured context from ID
via API. Feed your business identity into any tool that needs it.

## Integrations
- **Kinetiks Ecosystem**: Dark Madder, Harvest, Hypothesis, Litmus (native)
- **Fortune Farms**: Greenhaus personas sync with Audience Layer
- **CMS**: Framer (via Dark Madder)
- **Future**: API access for third-party tools

## Competitive Alternatives
| Alternative | Limitation |
|---|---|
| Notion brand wiki | Static, manual updates, no intelligence |
| HubSpot company profile | Flat data, no layered structure |
| Brand guidelines PDF | Dead on arrival, never consulted |
| ChatGPT custom instructions | Single flat context, no structure |

## Unique Value
ID is the only product that creates a structured, living business identity
designed to feed an entire marketing stack. It is not a document. It is not
a profile. It is an intelligence structure with dedicated operators that
maintain, validate, and evolve your identity continuously.
