# Harvest - Positioning

## Core Position
Outbound that doesn't feel like outbound.

## Expanded Positioning Statement
Harvest is the outbound engine in the Kinetiks ecosystem. It transforms
manual prospecting into a signal-driven pipeline machine - finding the
right contacts, building intelligent sequences, and managing deals from
first touch to close. Integrated with Bloomify for one-click prospecting
and powered by Kinetiks ID for deep business context, Harvest makes
outbound feel personal at scale.

## Target Market
- **Company stage**: B2B companies with 10-200 employees
- **Sales maturity**: Have SDRs or founders doing manual outreach
- **Current state**: Using spreadsheets, basic CRM, or cobbled-together tools
- **Decision maker**: Head of Sales, VP Sales, Sales Manager, founder-seller
- **Budget**: $200-1,000/mo for outbound tooling

## Core Differentiators
1. **Signal-Driven Prospecting**: Not random lists. Harvest identifies
   prospects based on buying signals, timing indicators, and ICP match
   from Kinetiks ID. Right person, right moment.
2. **Bloomify Chrome Extension**: One-click contact capture from LinkedIn,
   company websites, and directories. Enrichment happens automatically.
   Browse the web, build your pipeline.
3. **Intelligent Sequences**: Multi-step outbound sequences that adapt
   based on engagement. Not static drip campaigns - responsive cadences
   that adjust timing, channel, and messaging based on prospect behavior.
4. **Full Pipeline View**: Prospects, contacts, sequences, and deals in
   one view. No tab-switching between prospecting tool, email tool, and CRM.
5. **Kinetiks ID Integration**: Sequences pull from your business identity
   for personalization that goes beyond "Hi {first_name}."

## Primary Angle
The "manual outbound" problem. SDRs spend 70% of their time on research,
data entry, and tool-switching instead of selling. Harvest collapses that
workflow into a single engine where finding, reaching, and closing happen
in one place.

## Secondary Angles
- **The spam problem**: Most outbound tools optimize for volume. Harvest
  optimizes for relevance. Better targeting, better messaging, better replies.
- **The disconnected stack**: Prospecting in one tool, emailing in another,
  CRM in a third. Harvest unifies the outbound workflow.
- **The founder-seller bottleneck**: Founders doing outbound need maximum
  output from minimum time investment.

## Competitive Landscape
- **Apollo.io**: Large database, but sequence intelligence is basic
- **Outreach/Salesloft**: Enterprise-focused, complex, expensive
- **Lemlist**: Good personalization but weak on prospecting/pipeline
- **HubSpot Sales**: Broad CRM with shallow outbound features
- **Instantly.ai**: Volume-focused, deliverability over intelligence

## Key Messaging Framework
- **Awareness**: "Your SDRs spend 70% of their time not selling."
- **Consideration**: "One engine for prospecting, sequences, and pipeline."
- **Decision**: "Book more meetings in week one than your team did last month."
