# Harvest - Product

## Product Description
Harvest (hv.kinetiks.ai) is the outbound engine in the Kinetiks ecosystem.
It unifies prospecting, sequencing, and pipeline management into a single
workflow - replacing the fragmented stack of prospecting tools, email
platforms, and CRMs that sales teams currently juggle. Integrated with
the Bloomify Chrome extension for one-click contact capture and powered
by Kinetiks ID for intelligent personalization.

## Key Features

### 1. Prospects
Signal-driven prospect identification and management:
- ICP matching powered by Kinetiks ID audience data
- Buying signal detection for timing-aware outreach
- Prospect scoring and prioritization
- List building with enrichment and verification
- Import from CSV, LinkedIn, or Bloomify captures

### 2. Sequences
Intelligent multi-step outbound cadences:
- Email, LinkedIn, and multi-channel sequence steps
- Dynamic timing based on prospect engagement
- Personalization tokens powered by Kinetiks ID context
- A/B testing for subject lines, messaging, and cadence
- Automatic pause on reply or booking

### 3. Pipeline
Full deal tracking from first touch to close:
- Customizable pipeline stages
- Deal value tracking and forecasting
- Activity timeline for every contact
- Stage-based task automation
- Pipeline analytics and velocity metrics

### 4. Contacts
Centralized contact management with enrichment:
- Unified contact profiles across all touchpoints
- Automatic enrichment from Bloomify captures
- Engagement history and scoring
- Company and contact-level data
- Duplicate detection and merge

### 5. Bloomify Chrome Extension
One-click prospecting from anywhere on the web:
- Capture contacts from LinkedIn profiles and company pages
- Auto-enrich with email, phone, company data
- Add directly to sequences or prospect lists
- Works on any website with contact information
- Syncs instantly with Harvest pipeline

## Integrations
- **Kinetiks ID**: ICP data, personalization context (native)
- **Fortune Farms**: Shared Bloomify extension, Greenhaus personas
- **Email**: Gmail, Outlook for sequence sending
- **Calendar**: Google Calendar, Calendly for meeting booking
- **CRM**: HubSpot, Salesforce data sync (planned)

## Competitive Alternatives
| Alternative | Limitation |
|---|---|
| Apollo.io | Large database, basic sequence intelligence |
| Outreach | Enterprise pricing and complexity |
| Lemlist | Good personalization, weak pipeline management |
| Instantly.ai | Volume-focused, no CRM or pipeline |
| HubSpot Sales Hub | Broad CRM, shallow outbound features |

## Unique Value
Harvest is the only outbound engine that combines signal-driven prospecting,
intelligent sequences, and pipeline management in one tool - powered by deep
business context from Kinetiks ID. Other tools make you choose between
prospecting, sequencing, or CRM. Harvest is all three, and they talk to
each other.
