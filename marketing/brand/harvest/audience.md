# Harvest - Audience

## Primary Persona: "The Overworked SDR"

### Demographics
- **Title**: SDR, BDR, Account Executive doing their own prospecting
- **Company size**: 10-200 employees (B2B)
- **Industry**: SaaS, professional services, agencies, tech
- **Experience**: 1-5 years in sales, hungry to hit quota
- **Age range**: 23-32
- **Reports to**: Sales Manager, VP Sales, or founder directly

### Day-to-Day Reality
- Makes 50-80 outreach attempts per day across email and LinkedIn
- Spends 2-3 hours daily on manual research and data entry
- Switches between 4-6 tools: LinkedIn, prospecting tool, email, CRM, spreadsheet
- Personalizes emails by hand, one at a time, often poorly
- Knows the outbound is too generic but doesn't have time to fix it
- Measured on meetings booked and pipeline generated

### Pain Points (Ranked)
1. **Time on non-selling activities**: Research, data entry, and tool-switching eat the day
2. **Low reply rates**: Generic outbound gets ignored. Personalization takes too long.
3. **Fragmented workflow**: Prospecting, emailing, and tracking happen in separate tools
4. **Data quality**: Contact info is often wrong, outdated, or incomplete
5. **Sequence rigidity**: Current tools send the same drip regardless of engagement signals

### Sophistication Level
- **Sales knowledge**: Understands pipeline stages, qualification frameworks, objection handling.
- **Technical**: Comfortable with Chrome extensions, CRM basics, email tools.
  Not technical enough for API integrations or complex automations.
- **Outbound literacy**: Knows what good outbound looks like but lacks the tools
  and time to execute it consistently.

### What They Value
- Speed. Every minute saved is another prospect reached.
- Reply rates over send volume.
- Tools that require minimal setup and training.
- Anything that makes their pipeline look fuller in Monday's standup.
- Mobile-friendly interfaces for on-the-go pipeline checks.

### Where They Hang Out
- **LinkedIn**: Primary hunting ground and professional network
- **Communities**: RevGenius, Sales Hacker, Bravado
- **Podcasts**: 30 Minutes to President's Club, Surf and Sales
- **Content**: Follow outbound influencers on LinkedIn and Twitter
- **Tools they already use**: LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Apollo, HubSpot

## Secondary Persona: "The Founder-Seller"
- CEO or co-founder at a 5-30 person company doing outbound themselves
- Has no sales team yet - they are the sales team
- Needs maximum efficiency from minimum time investment
- Less interested in features, more interested in meetings booked
- Values Bloomify for quick-capture while browsing potential customers
- Will upgrade to a team plan once they hire their first SDR
