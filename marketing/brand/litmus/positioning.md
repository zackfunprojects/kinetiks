# Litmus - Positioning

## Core Position
Earn attention. Don't buy it.

## Expanded Positioning Statement
Litmus is the PR engine in the Kinetiks ecosystem. It replaces the need for
an expensive PR agency by providing a multi-agent system that manages journalist
relationships, crafts targeted pitches, tracks media coverage, and runs PR
campaigns - all powered by deep business context from Kinetiks ID. Built for
founders and marketing leads who know press matters but can't justify agency fees.

## Target Market
- **Company stage**: Startups and scale-ups, 10-300 employees
- **PR maturity**: Know they need press, have tried DIY with poor results
- **Current state**: No PR agency, maybe one failed agency engagement
- **Decision maker**: Founder, CEO, Head of Marketing, Head of Comms
- **Budget**: $200-800/mo (vs $5,000-15,000/mo for a PR agency)

## Core Differentiators
1. **Multi-Agent PR System**: Not one chatbot - a system of specialized agents
   that handle different PR functions: journalist research, pitch crafting,
   campaign management, coverage tracking, and relationship maintenance.
2. **Journalist Intelligence**: Deep profiles on journalists including their
   beat, recent articles, pitch preferences, response patterns, and timing.
   Pitches are matched to journalists, not blasted to lists.
3. **Story-First Approach**: Litmus starts with story development before
   pitching. It identifies newsworthy angles from your Kinetiks ID context
   and shapes them into pitches journalists actually want to cover.
4. **Agency-Grade at DIY Price**: Provides the strategic framework, journalist
   relationships, and campaign management that agencies charge $10K/mo for,
   at a fraction of the cost.
5. **Coverage Intelligence**: Tracks mentions, articles, and media sentiment
   across publications. Measures PR impact beyond vanity impressions.

## Primary Angle
The "PR agency gap." Companies between $2M and $50M in revenue need press
coverage but can't justify $10-15K/month agency retainers. Litmus fills that
gap with an intelligent PR engine that delivers agency-level strategy at
self-serve pricing.

## Secondary Angles
- **The spray-and-pray problem**: Most DIY PR is sending generic press releases
  to massive lists. Litmus replaces volume with precision.
- **The relationship gap**: PR works on relationships. Without an agency,
  most companies have zero journalist relationships. Litmus builds them.
- **The story problem**: Companies pitch products, but journalists want stories.
  Litmus identifies the story angles that earn coverage.

## Competitive Landscape
- **PR agencies**: Expensive ($5-15K/mo), slow, opaque about process
- **Prowly/Muck Rack**: Journalist databases, not strategy engines
- **Cision**: Enterprise PR suite, complex and expensive
- **HARO/Qwoted**: Reactive opportunities only, no proactive pitching
- **Press release distribution (PRWeb, BusinessWire)**: Broadcast, not targeted

## Key Messaging Framework
- **Awareness**: "PR agencies charge $10K/month. Most of it is process you can systematize."
- **Consideration**: "A multi-agent PR system that knows your story and your journalists."
- **Decision**: "Land your first earned coverage within 30 days of starting."
