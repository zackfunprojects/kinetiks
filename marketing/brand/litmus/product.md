# Litmus - Product

## Product Description
Litmus (lt.kinetiks.ai) is the PR engine in the Kinetiks ecosystem. It
provides a multi-agent system that handles the full earned media workflow:
journalist research and relationship management, story angle development,
pitch crafting, campaign orchestration, and coverage tracking. Powered by
Kinetiks ID for deep business context, Litmus delivers PR agency-grade
strategy and execution at self-serve pricing.

## Key Features

### 1. Journalists
Intelligent journalist database and relationship management:
- Journalist profiles with beat, publication, recent articles, and pitch preferences
- Match scoring based on relevance to your story angles
- Relationship tracking: pitch history, response rates, coverage generated
- Journalist discovery for new beat areas and publications
- Contact enrichment and verification

### 2. Pitches
Story-first pitch development powered by multi-agent system:
- Story angle identification from Kinetiks ID business context
- Pitch crafting tailored to individual journalist preferences
- Multiple pitch variants for A/B approach testing
- Timing recommendations based on news cycle and journalist patterns
- Follow-up sequence management with smart spacing

### 3. Campaigns
Structured PR campaign management:
- Campaign planning with goals, timelines, and target publications
- Multi-journalist outreach coordination
- Campaign performance tracking (pitches sent, responses, placements)
- Asset management for press kits, images, and supporting materials
- Campaign templates for common PR plays (launch, funding, milestone)

### 4. Articles & Mentions
Coverage tracking and media monitoring:
- Automated mention detection across publications and online media
- Article archiving with sentiment analysis
- Coverage reporting for stakeholder updates
- Share of voice tracking against competitors
- Media mention alerts and notifications

### 5. Multi-Agent PR System
Specialized agents that handle different PR functions:
- **Research Agent**: Identifies relevant journalists and maps media landscape
- **Story Agent**: Develops newsworthy angles from business context
- **Pitch Agent**: Crafts targeted pitches matched to journalist preferences
- **Campaign Agent**: Orchestrates outreach timing and follow-up sequences
- **Monitor Agent**: Tracks coverage, mentions, and media sentiment

## Integrations
- **Kinetiks ID**: Business context, positioning, news angles (native)
- **Dark Madder**: Press-ready content and thought leadership pieces (native)
- **Email**: Gmail, Outlook for pitch delivery
- **Monitoring**: Google Alerts, social listening feeds
- **Analytics**: Coverage impact on web traffic and lead generation
- **CRM**: Link coverage to pipeline influence (via Harvest)

## Unique Value
Litmus is the only PR tool built as a multi-agent system that handles the
full earned media workflow from story development to coverage tracking.
Other tools give you a journalist database or a distribution channel.
Litmus gives you the strategic layer that agencies charge $10K/month for:
identify the story, match the journalist, craft the pitch, run the campaign,
and measure the coverage - all powered by your Kinetiks ID business context.
