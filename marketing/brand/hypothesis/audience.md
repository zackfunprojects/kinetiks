# Hypothesis - Audience

## Primary Persona: "The Blocked Growth Marketer"

### Demographics
- **Title**: Growth Marketer, Demand Gen Manager, Performance Marketing Lead
- **Company size**: 20-500 employees (growth stage)
- **Industry**: B2B SaaS, fintech, edtech, marketplace businesses
- **Experience**: 3-8 years in growth/performance marketing
- **Age range**: 26-36
- **Reports to**: VP Marketing, Head of Growth, CMO

### Day-to-Day Reality
- Runs paid campaigns across Google, LinkedIn, Meta - landing pages are the bottleneck
- Has a backlog of 10+ landing page requests waiting on engineering
- Creates pages in tools that weren't designed for testing, then bolts on A/B tools
- Makes page decisions based on "what the VP liked" instead of data
- Knows they should be testing more but the setup cost is too high
- Judges success by CAC, conversion rates, and pipeline generated

### Pain Points (Ranked)
1. **Engineering dependency**: Every landing page change requires a developer ticket
   and a 2-week wait. Campaign timing suffers.
2. **Testing friction**: Setting up an A/B test is a project in itself. Most pages
   ship untested because it's too much overhead.
3. **No statistical rigor**: When tests do run, winners are called too early.
   Decisions are made on noise, not signal.
4. **Template limitations**: Current tools offer generic templates that don't match
   brand or require heavy customization.
5. **Disconnected insights**: Page performance data lives in analytics tools,
   completely disconnected from content and outbound strategy.

### Sophistication Level
- **Growth expertise**: Deep understanding of funnels, conversion optimization,
  attribution, and experimentation frameworks.
- **Technical**: Comfortable with no-code tools, analytics platforms, tag managers.
  Can write basic HTML/CSS but shouldn't have to.
- **Statistical literacy**: Understands p-values and confidence intervals conceptually.
  Wants tools that handle the math correctly so they don't have to.

### What They Value
- Speed to live. Hours, not weeks.
- Statistical validity over gut feelings.
- Independence from engineering for marketing pages.
- Clean data that connects to their analytics stack.
- Tools that respect their intelligence without requiring a PhD.

### Where They Hang Out
- **Communities**: Reforge, CXL, GrowthHackers, Demand Curve
- **LinkedIn**: Active in growth marketing circles
- **Newsletters**: Lenny's Newsletter, Growth Unhinged, The Growth Newsletter
- **Podcasts**: Lenny's Podcast, How I Built This, Growth TLDRs
- **Conferences**: SaaStr, Growth Marketing Conference, CXL Live

## Secondary Persona: "The Solo Performance Marketer"
- Only marketer at a 10-30 person startup
- Runs everything: ads, landing pages, email, analytics
- Cannot afford to wait on anyone for anything
- Values templates and speed over customization depth
- Will pay for a tool that saves 5 hours per week on page creation
- Less focused on statistical rigor, more on rapid iteration
