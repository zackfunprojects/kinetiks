# Hypothesis - Positioning

## Core Position
Test everything. Trust nothing until the data speaks.

## Expanded Positioning Statement
Hypothesis is the landing page engine in the Kinetiks ecosystem. It enables
growth teams to create, deploy, and A/B test landing pages without engineering
help - turning every page into a measurable experiment. Connected to Kinetiks
ID for brand-consistent messaging and feeding results back into the ecosystem
for compounding intelligence.

## Target Market
- **Company stage**: Scale-ups and growth-stage startups, 20-500 employees
- **Growth maturity**: Running paid campaigns, need landing pages fast
- **Current pain**: Waiting on engineering for page builds and test setup
- **Decision maker**: Growth marketer, demand gen lead, performance marketing manager
- **Budget**: $200-800/mo for landing page and testing tools

## Core Differentiators
1. **Test-First Architecture**: Every page is built as an experiment from
   the start. A/B testing is not an add-on feature - it's the foundation.
   You don't build a page, you build a hypothesis.
2. **No-Code Page Builder**: Growth marketers build and deploy landing pages
   without touching engineering. Templates, drag-and-drop sections, and
   component libraries purpose-built for conversion testing.
3. **Statistical Rigor**: Real confidence intervals, sample size calculations,
   and significance testing. Not vanity metrics - actual statistical validity
   that you can trust for decision-making.
4. **Template Intelligence**: Templates informed by Kinetiks ID context.
   Pages pre-populated with your positioning, value props, and audience
   language. Not blank canvases - intelligent starting points.
5. **Ecosystem Feedback**: Test results feed back into the Kinetiks ecosystem.
   Winning messaging patterns inform Dark Madder content. Audience insights
   update Kinetiks ID. Every test teaches the whole stack.

## Primary Angle
The "engineering bottleneck" problem. Growth marketers have hypotheses they
want to test right now, but landing page creation and A/B test setup requires
engineering time they don't have. Hypothesis removes the bottleneck entirely.

## Secondary Angles
- **The untested assumption**: Most landing pages are launched on gut feeling
  and never tested. Hypothesis makes testing the default, not the exception.
- **The vanity metrics trap**: Most A/B testing tools declare winners before
  reaching statistical significance. Hypothesis enforces real math.
- **The disconnected page**: Landing pages built in isolation from brand
  context produce inconsistent messaging. Hypothesis pulls from Kinetiks ID.

## Competitive Landscape
- **Unbounce**: Established but bloated. Testing is secondary to building.
- **Instapage**: Enterprise-focused, expensive, complex personalization
- **Leadpages**: SMB-focused, limited testing capabilities
- **Webflow**: Powerful builder, no native A/B testing
- **Google Optimize**: Sunset. The market needs a replacement.
- **VWO/Optimizely**: Full CRO suites, overkill for landing page testing

## Key Messaging Framework
- **Awareness**: "Your landing page is an untested opinion. Make it an experiment."
- **Consideration**: "Build, test, and iterate landing pages without engineering."
- **Decision**: "Launch your first A/B test in under 15 minutes. Get results in days."
